The user is the founder and product owner of MindScrolling. Has a strong sense for content quality and authenticity — does not want motivational poster clichés or misattributed quotes. Values philosophical depth and accurate attribution above volume.

Understands at a product level what makes a quote "good" for the app (philosophical, reflective, timeless). Trusts the Data Engineer to own quality scoring and attribution verification independently.

Expects the agent to proactively flag attribution errors, not wait to be asked. Reports should be concrete: N quotes changed, distribution breakdown, quality averages.
