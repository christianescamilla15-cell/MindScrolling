## Full Description (max 4000 chars)

### English

```
What if every scroll made you wiser?

MindScrolling replaces mindless doom-scrolling with a curated feed of 13,000+ philosophical quotes spanning Stoicism, Existentialism, Eastern Philosophy, and Modern Thought. Instead of draining your attention, every swipe builds your intellectual profile.

HOW IT WORKS
Swipe in 4 directions -- each one means something:
- Swipe RIGHT to like a quote
- Swipe LEFT to skip
- Swipe UP to save to your Vault
- Swipe DOWN to reflect deeper

The app learns what resonates with you. Powered by pgvector AI recommendations, your feed adapts over time -- surfacing quotes that challenge and inspire you personally. This is not a random quote generator.

KEY FEATURES

Feed That Learns
Our adaptive algorithm tracks your philosophical preferences across 4 categories and fine-tunes your feed. The more you swipe, the smarter it gets.

Philosophy Map
Watch your intellectual profile evolve. The Philosophy Map visualizes which schools of thought you gravitate toward -- Stoicism, Existentialism, Eastern wisdom, or Modern philosophy -- and how your taste shifts over time.

Vault
Save the quotes that hit hardest. Your Vault stores every quote you swipe up on, organized and searchable. Build your personal library of wisdom.

Daily Challenge
One quote per day, designed to push you outside your comfort zone. Reflect, journal, and grow.

Streaks & Reflections
Track your consistency. Daily streaks and reflection counters keep you engaged without gamifying your attention away.

Bilingual (EN/ES)
Full support for English and Spanish. The app detects your device language and serves quotes accordingly.

PREMIUM ($4.99 one-time purchase)
- Unlock the full 13,000+ quote library
- Advanced Philosophy Map analytics
- Exclusive content packs
- No subscriptions, no recurring fees -- pay once, own it forever

PRIVACY FIRST
- Minimal data collection
- No ad tracking
- No social graph harvesting
- Your philosophical journey stays yours

Built for people who want their screen time to mean something.
```
